About NYSL

Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn an practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship

Vision

The Northeside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletes throug the building o character, self-discipline, and leadership

General Information

The Northeside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in witch to learn play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside in a Chiago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran a small full-time staff, an relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members
